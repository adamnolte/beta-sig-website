-President's Corner-

After another great spring semester in the books – hosting an Apple watch philanthropy raffle, increasing our overall GPA by 0.2, and being one of the first Fraternities at the University of Missouri to achieve accreditation – Beta Sigs are enjoying their summer off, or working at their internships around the country, with companies such as Anheuser Busch in St. Louis and Fox News in New York City. If you are interested in rushing Beta Sigma Psi in the coming Fall or Spring semester, please don’t hesitate to contact one of our recruitment chairs or myself, which are currently working hard at recruiting our Fall 2019 Pledge class in St. Louis, Kansas City, and Columbia. While many Beta Sigs are away from Columbia during the summer we are very much looking forward to having everyone back just in time for IFC formal recruitment on August 14-17. Sign-ups for formal recruitment can be found here https://fsl.missouri.edu/ifc/ifc-recruitment/. We are pleased to be welcoming a familiar but yet new face to our organization this year. Our current chef, Justin Jackson will be starting his first semester as our house dad, and we couldn’t be more excited to have him in a new role!
