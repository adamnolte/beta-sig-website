-President's Corner-

Following an amazing semester full of meeting new people, continuing our academic success, and growing in brotherhood, Beta Sigs can’t wait to get back to Mizzou in January to welcome our winter semester class of new members. We’ve been working hard to adapt our culture to fit the new standards for Greek Life and are excited to see what opportunities the new year will bring. We are saying goodbye for a semester to some of our brothers as we welcome home others who have been studying abroad this past fall, and can’t wait to hear about the experiences that they have had. If you have interest in becoming a member of Beta Sigma Psi in the upcoming spring or fall semesters, please feel free to reach out to one of our Recruitment Chairmen or myself for any questions that you may have. With the growing excitement for our brand-new philanthropy event, as well as Greek Week being just a few short months away, it’s a great time to be a Beta Sig and we would love to meet you and help you get the most out of your Greek life experience.
