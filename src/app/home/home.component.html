-President's Corner-

After a successful semester, Beta Sigs are currently enjoying summer in Columbia or back at their respective homes, but can’t wait to get back to 206 S College Ave for Formal Recruitment June 23-25 and the beginning of the Fall semester in August. All of our focus over this summer is being put towards our recruitment process and improvements to the chapter house.

With recruitment chairs in the Kansas City, the St. Louis and now the Chicago areas we are able to reach out to more potential new members than ever before. We have multiple events scheduled throughout the summer in each of those cities. If you are interested in rushing simply contact any of the rush chairs or myself. Sign ups for formal recruitment can be found here at https://greeklife.missouri.edu/ifc/ifc-recruitment/.

With formal rush coming up we have been working hard to make multiple renovations to the house. Enhancements are currently being made to the foyer, study room, bathrooms, and various smaller projects throughout the house. All of these changes will be ready by Formal Recruitment, putting the house in top shape for all of the rushees coming through that weekend. We’re all excited for the FS17 associate member class and can’t wait to get back to campus in August!

Eric Graves, President
